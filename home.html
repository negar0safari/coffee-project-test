Espresso

Espresso is made by passing very hot water (about 95 degrees Celsius) under pressure through ground and compacted coffee.

Mocha

Caff√® mocha or Mocaccino is a chocolate type of cafe latte. Like a cafe latte, it is prepared based on espresso and hot milk, but sweet chocolate, often in the form of cocoa powder, is added to it.

Hot Chocolate

Hot chocolate is a hot drink that typically includes grated or melted chocolate or cocoa powder, heated milk or water, and sugar.

Americano

An Americano is made by combining hot water with espresso. The ratio for an Americano is typically one or two shots of espresso (30 to 60 cc of espresso) and twice the volume of hot water, but everything depends on your taste. To reduce the intensity of espresso coffee, we can add more hot water.

Milkshake

The same recipe is used to prepare the best milkshakes available in cafes. The combination of this popular drink includes various ingredients such as ice cream, cream, milk, and sugar. Of course, other additives such as fruits and vegetables can also be used to prepare milkshakes.
